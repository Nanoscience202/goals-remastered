Due Date

A goal must be timed. You need to feel pressured to complete your goal on time. It must be a numerical date.

Specific Goal

A good goal must be concrete. You need a clear sense of what you're going to accomplish. For this section, think of how it could be measured and assign an end value to it. It must be realistic and meaningful.

Concrete Plan

A goal contains many smaller goals. Break it up into smaller goals and assign a due date to them. It will help with motivation as well as keeping track of your progress. A concrete plan is essential for success.
